Summersite: Venetian Light

Photography Workshop: 12 days
May 31-June 12, 2001

Taught By: Holly Smith Pedlosky
Teaching Assistant: Roslyn Banish

 

Come to Venice in June at the start of a Biennale summer: a chance to study photography intensely and use the camera as a tool to explore an extraordinary place, of which the 16th c. diplomat Philippe de Commines wrote: "It is the most triumphant city that I have ever seen."
