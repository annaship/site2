Varenna and the Castle of Vezio from the Eremo

Nuts & Bolts

Cost: $ 3,550 per person includes 12 nights’ lodging, double occupancy with private bath
and generous Continental breakfast, first night reception and dinner, a supper in a mountain tavern, maid service, gratuities, tuition, Holly’s booklet of useful information about Varenna, including tips about how to find the best gelato and tiramisu, and a Northern Italian Lakeside banquet on the last evening.
The fee does not include airfare or photo processing.
Early registrants have a better chance of securing a room which has a spectacular view of Lake Como and the surrounding mountains. A few single rooms are available, for a supplemental fee of $500, on a first-come first-served basis. Companions are welcome, on a space-available basis, for $2,550, which includes lodging, group meals, and field trips.
