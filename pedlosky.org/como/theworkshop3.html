Lake Como from the Castle of Vezio

The Workshop

This is a workshop for all levels and types of photographers, beginner through advanced, traditional and digital. Students will be divided into groups according to skill level. In the morning and afternoon sessions, we will have demonstrations, slide talks, and critiques. As a group we will take field trips, exploring villages along Lake Como and the hill towns above it. There will be individual conferences with the instructors and ample free time to make photographs.

You will need a 35mm camera, preferably a film or digital single lens reflex, with some manual control over aperture and shutter speed. Other camera formats are welcome.
